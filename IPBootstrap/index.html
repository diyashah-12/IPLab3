About Us

ALPHABET is a school in Andheri, Mumbai, India. It is one of top school in Andheri. It has alumni across industries including the Bollywood film industry, cricket, television, music and authors. ALPHABET was established in 1954 by the Redemptorists. The school campus hosts contemporary facilities to support the academic and extracurricular pursuits of the students. These include spacious classrooms with audio visual aid, state-of-the-art science and computer laboratories, well stocked library featuring a huge collection of books on various subjects and a canteen for students within the school campus.
